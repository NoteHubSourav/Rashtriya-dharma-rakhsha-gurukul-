About Our Gurukul

Rashtriya Dharma Raksha Gurukul is dedicated to reviving traditional education and moral values. We offer programs in philosophy, yoga, Sanskrit, and cultural arts. Our goal is to instill discipline and spiritual wisdom in students of all backgrounds.

Our Mission

Our mission is to safeguard Dharma (righteousness) by educating future generations. We focus on holistic development – intellectual, physical, and spiritual. By integrating modern knowledge with ancient wisdom, we aim to build a community rooted in harmony and service.
