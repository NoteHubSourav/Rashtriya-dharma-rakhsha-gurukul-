Our Mission & Vision

At Rashtriya Dharma Raksha Gurukul, our sacred mission is to provide a loving home and a holistic education to orphaned children. We believe in preserving and imparting the rich heritage of Gurukul-based learning, fostering character, knowledge, and spiritual growth to empower the next generation.

Our vision is to cultivate responsible citizens deeply rooted in Sanatana Dharma, equipped with both ancient wisdom and modern skills, ready to lead with integrity and compassion.

Our Impact & Programs

Your support directly impacts the lives of children by providing:

Holistic Education

Integrating Vedic studies, Yoga, Sanskrit, and traditional arts with modern academic subjects like Math, Science, and English.

Safe Shelter & Care

A nurturing, family-like environment with comfortable living spaces, nutritious meals, and essential medical support.

Cultural & Moral Values

Instilling strong ethical principles, discipline, and a deep understanding of Indian culture and spiritual heritage.

Skill Development

Equipping children with practical life skills and vocational training to ensure self-reliance and successful integration into society.
