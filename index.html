About the Gurukul

Rashtriya Dharma Raksha Gurukul is devoted to reviving Sanatan Dharma by providing orphans with traditional Vedic education, discipline, and a spiritual lifestyle.

Why Support Us

Your support helps preserve cultural values, offer hope to orphans, and build protectors of Dharma for future generations.

Orphans & Education

We raise abandoned children into responsible, dharmic protectors through gurukul-style learning and care. Our mission is holistic development of body, mind, and spirit.
